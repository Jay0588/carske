Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping within the United States typically takes 3-7 business days. Express shipping takes 2-3 business days, and Next Day Delivery is available for orders placed before 2pm (recipient's local time).

International shipping times vary by location, generally taking 7-14 business days.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn, unwashed items in their original condition with tags attached. Returns are free for customers in the United States. International customers are responsible for return shipping costs.

To initiate a return, please visit our Returns Portal or contact our customer support team.

How do I find my size?

We provide detailed size guides on each product page. Our t-shirts generally run true to size, but we recommend checking the specific measurements provided for each style.

If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more relaxed fit or sizing down for a more fitted look.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 30 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. Please note that customers are responsible for any customs fees or import duties that may apply.

How do I care for my Carske t-shirt?

For best results, machine wash cold with like colors, using a gentle cycle. Tumble dry low or lay flat to dry. Avoid using bleach or fabric softeners, as these can affect the quality of the fabric and print.

For detailed care instructions, please refer to the care label on your specific item.
